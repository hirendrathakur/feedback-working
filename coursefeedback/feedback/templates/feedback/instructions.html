STUDENT'S FEEDBACK ON COURSES

EVEN SEMESTER 2014-15

  • Report a problem
  • Instructions

Mail: student.feedback@itbhu.ac.in


1. Enter your Institute email address and click submit. If @iitbhu.ac.in doesn't work use @itbhu.ac.in.

2. On submission, a system generated email will be sent to your Institute email address with the link for giving your feedback on courses that you have undergone during the recently concluded semester.

3. Click on the link sent to your gmail, then click on the name of a particular professor from the list of courses on the course dashboard page.

4. Fill the checkboxes of questions then click on "Submit Feedback".

5. ......and it's Done.

If you are facing any problem with feedback portal for e.g. unable to receive mail, course is not listed etc. feel free to contact us at student.feedback@itbhu.ac.in
